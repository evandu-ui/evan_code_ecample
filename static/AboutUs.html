ABOUT US

The Photographic Angle (TPA) is a charitable organisation with a dedicated team of permanent staff members who ensure that all of our exhibitions are curated, marketed, and installed to a high and consistent standard.

TEAM TRUSTEES AMBASSADORS

TEAM

Each member of the team brings with them a particular skill set that helps to transform otherwise unused buildings into venues that bring photography to life. We aim to enable the public to engage with our exhibition themes whilst at the same time gaining some valuable knowledge that can help them to improve their own photography skills and enhance their appreciation of the art and science of photography.

TRUSTEES

The Photographic Angle and the work that we do is overseen by our board of trustees who lend their skills and experience to our organisation. The majority are now retired but have previously held prominent positions in the worlds of academia, commerce, the professions and the arts.

AMBASSADORS

As our exhibitions tour around the country, we have created a comprehensive network of local representatives who are based in and around the areas where we host our events. They are able to use their knowledge of the local area in order to help us market and advertise our exhibitions to potential visitors. They also oversee the installation of the photographs at our various venues ensuring that each exhibition is in place and ready to receive visitors.
