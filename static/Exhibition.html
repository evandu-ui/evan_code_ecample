The Photographic Angle (TPA) stages photographic exhibitions that travel across the UK transforming otherwise unused spaces into temporary galleries. The exhibitions are open to the general public and admission is free. Each venue is open from 10am to 3pm daily on the dates specified.

Group visits are welcomed and can be arranged by contacting Hannah Clegg on 01803 837896 or by emailing hannah@thephotographicangle.co.uk

Disabled visitors are advised to check with us before visiting so that suitable access can be arranged.

Details of all our exhibition venues across the country can be found below. Simply click on the venue you are interested in to find directions, detailed information about access to the building, and a small selection of photographs of the inside of the building.

For information on our exhibition themes and to see a small sample of the images that we exhibit, please see our Gallery page.
